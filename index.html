Sally Bradfield is a native of Colorado that has traveled all over the continental US, Mexico and Canada. She is studying full-stack web development at the University of Denver and is currently working to complete the certificate program. Sally is passionate about web design and loves experimenting with different technologies. Aditionally, Sally has a Bachelor's of Science in Information Technology from Colorado State University and worked for nearly five years as a Quality Assurance Analyst at Urban Lending.

On her spare Sally enjoys outdoor activities like hiking, biking, camping, boating, jet-skiing and wakeboarding. Winter is her favoriate season because she loves enduring the elements while snowshoeing, skiing and camping well into fall. while indoors she loves her menagerie of cats, a couple dogs and birds. Sally enjoys cooking and cleaning for her lazy, boring husband.
